40 Days
Crusade

The 40 Days Crusade is an annual event that takes place at Calvary Temple Worship Centre. It is a time of spiritual renewal, prayer, and fasting for the church community.

Over the 40 days, thousands of lives are transformed as individuals encounter renewed hope, deepened faith, and lasting community support. Through committed prayer, powerful teaching, and heartfelt fellowship, people experience healing, restored relationships, and a renewed sense of purpose that continues long after the event.

Join us for heartfelt worship, prayer, and renewal. Expect inspiring teaching, powerful worship, and times of personal reflection and community support. All are welcome to experience transformation, healing, and renewed purpose.
