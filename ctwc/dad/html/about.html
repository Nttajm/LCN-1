About
Us

Calvary Temple Worship Centre started in 2004 in the suburb of Mbale town in a Muslim community of Namakwekwe. The church began with 7 people and now grown to a membership of 1200+ congregants. read more

Later as a way of expanding our reach into the community, which could not just be done at church level, we were mandated to register our ministry in the name of Champion Evangelistic Ministry International which was successful done in 2010. The registration is as well a requirement of government to permit church operations beyond the church building. The ministry is fully registered and hosted at Calvary Temple worship Centre, main base in Mbale.
